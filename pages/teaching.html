Teaching Philosophy & Awards

I believe in fostering critical thinking and hands-on learning experiences that prepare students for real-world challenges in AI and computer science. My teaching emphasizes the ethical implications of AI technologies and the importance of responsible research practices.

I strive to create inclusive learning environments where all students can succeed, regardless of their background or prior experience. Through a combination of theoretical foundations and practical applications, I aim to inspire the next generation of AI researchers and practitioners.
